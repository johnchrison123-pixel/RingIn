# RingIn VC Research Report — Fundraising 2024-2026

## 1. Voice / Audio Social Startups

### Airchat — launched April 2024
- Self-funded by Naval Ravikant + Brian Norgard (Tinder ex-CPO)
- Async voice-first; auto-transcribed; #27 in Apple social networking at launch
- **Thesis:** Voice conveys tone, humanity, nuance text loses. Async removes performance pressure.
- **RingIn shares:** Voice-first belief. **Differs:** Live synchronous, not async posting. Monetization built in.

### Wispr (Flow) — $81M total, Nov 2025
- $25M from Notable Capital + Steven Bartlett's Flight Fund; prior $30M Menlo. Total $81M. $700M post-money.
- Voice-to-text OS-layer dictation (not social, but validates voice as input)
- ElevenLabs: $80M Series B (Jan 2024) → $180M Series C at $3.3B (2025). Lightspeed at $300M+ ARR.

2. "Ad-supported social" — Want monetization day one

## Top 5 Day-1 Monetization Tactics VCs Love

**1. Virtual Gifts / Coin Economy**
Users buy coins, spend on hosts during live sessions. Platform 30-50%.
- Precedent: TikTok coins $6B 2024. FRND ₹198Cr ARR. Whatnot $8B GMV.
- Start day 1. Even 100 DAU with 5% buying coins = monetization curve.

**2. Paid 1:1 Access (Expert / Host Mode)**
Experts set per-minute or per-session price. Platform 20-30%.
- Precedent: FRND 25-30% creators earn real money. Passes ($40M Series A).
- **VCs love:** High-ARPU. Supply-side incentive without paying upfront. Self-selects engaged users.

**3. Subscription / Membership Tier**
$5-20/month premium (higher call quality, priority matching, exclusive hosts).
- Precedent: Bluesky $15M Series A with subs announced. Passes 90/10. AI companion apps $8-20/month = $120M sector ARR 2025.
- **VCs want:** 50 users at $9.99/month = willingness-to-pay proof.

**4. Creator Revenue Share (Retention + Supply Moat)**
Pay creators weekly. Public leaderboards. Payout = social proof for recruiting creators.
- Precedent: FRND creator payout built supply moat. Fanfix $100M ARR. Passes paid "nine figures."
- **VCs see:** Negative CAC for supply.

**5. Anonymous / Discovery Mode as Paid Unlock**
Anonymous discovery free; revealing profile, saving contact, extending beyond 3 minutes requires coins.
- Precedent: Bumble premium, Wink. RingIn-specific — matches anonymous-connect feature.
- **VCs love:** Natural paywall at highest-intent moment.
